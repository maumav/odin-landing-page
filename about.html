About Us

We are a group of experienced counsellors and therapists who strive to validate each person's experieces and find a cooperative resolution.
We strive to help you process your complex emotions, memories, experiences, disoders, mania, trauma and other psychological dysfunctions.
Our goal is to help you find normalcy in your everyday life despite carrying heavy mental weights.

Your Rights

You have rights protected under Health and Disability Act of Carthage and Pychotherapists Board of Carthage.

Fees

Fees are covered under Health and Disability if referred by your primary physician.
Fees for private counselling differrs depending on the counsellor/therapist and type of therapy taken.
Please contact us for more information regarding fees.
